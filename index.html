Akan Names Generator


Across many cultures in the world, naming of a person has always been informed by several factors. Some of them include the day of birth, time of the day one is born, season of the year or even after a certain personality.

Here I have built an interactive program that interpretes and shows a name from the Ghanaian naming methods called Akan.

Akan names are derived from Ghanaian culture.In Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

This program is designed to allow users check in their date and year of birth to get their Akan names.
Give it a try below. Simply input your date of birth and gender and see the magic. Enjoy the experience!!
